Brunch Spots

  • Decades - Anaheim
    • Decades features live music and bottomless mimosas.
    • The best way to find out about their events is through their Facebook page.
  • Snooze - Orange; Tustin
    • Snooze is a hip and cozy breakfast spot that features craft cocktails and rotating pancakes of the month.
    • Yelp manages the waitlist for both restaurants, and is highly recommended.
